How Does It Work?

JamSync allows you to host a room, or alternatively join a room, and play music on multiple devices to create one loud, synchronized, and affordable speaker system. All members of the room can control what song to play next, resync the music, or play a song over again!

Why Do You Need It?

Whether you're part of a flash mob, at a house party, on the golf course, or just in dire need to spread your infectious music, JamSync is here to make it all possible!
